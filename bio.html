Hello there,my name is Ntsebo Hlajoane and welcome again to my about me page.I am a Mosotho woamn aged twenty-three years of age.My passion is everything involving creativity eversince I was little. I pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Systems and soon after earning my degree, I volunteered at the Girls Coding Week by Vodacom Foundation.Currently, I am professioanally working Office Administrator and Trainer at BasaliTech.BasaliTech PTY is a Lesotho registred non profit organisation that exixts to provide affordable opportunities for women and children interested in learning Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics(STEM) through in-person classes, workshops, seminars, and community support. This is to protect their futures by introducing them to a growing field industry and to address a social challenge of gender gap in STEM careers.I did minor Business Administration courses and major Computer Science courses,but most of the things I have learned are self-taught through online tutorials and constant self-study.
